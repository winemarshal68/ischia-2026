Getting around Ischia — buses, taxis & the Epomeo hike

Public Bus · EAV (Ente Autonomo Volturno)
Island Bus Network
CS — Circolare Sinistra
Anti-clockwise route
Ischia Porto → Casamicciola → Lacco Ameno → Forio → Cava Grado → Serrara → Fontana → Barano → Ischia Porto
✓ Takes CS from Forio to Fontana for Epomeo
CD — Circolare Destra
Clockwise route
Ischia Porto → Barano → Fontana → Serrara → Cava Grado → Forio → Lacco Ameno → Casamicciola → Ischia Porto
✓ Also reaches Fontana (longer route from Forio via Ischia Porto)
Ticket price€1.50 single (on bus: €2.00) · Day pass €4.50 · Weekly pass ~€15.50
Buy ticketsTabaccheria (tobacconist) · bars · newspaper stands · hotel · at the bus terminal at Ischia Porto. Buy in advance — not always available on board.
ValidateAlways stamp your ticket in the machine on the bus — fine if you don't
Hours04:20–01:50 (summer) — very frequent during the day
From ForioBus stop near the port / Piazza Municipio area. Ask at hotel for exact stop.
To FontanaTake CS from Forio towards Fontana — ~30min · €1.50pp. Alight at Fontana church (large white statue of Jesus)
Other linesLine 1 (Ischia Porto–Forio–Sant'Angelo) · Line 2 (Ischia Porto–Forio–Citara/Poseidon) · Line 21/22/23/24 (Forio local routes)

Hike · Monday 29 June
Monte Epomeo (789m)
🥾 Planned — Monday 29 June
Option A — Bus to Fontana (recommended)
Step 1Walk to Forio bus stop · take CS bus towards Fontana (~30min · €1.50pp)
Step 2Get off at Fontana — look for the church with the large white statue of Jesus
Step 3Cross the road, turn left, follow signs to Monte Epomeo (3km · ~1hr uphill)
Step 4Summit! Visit San Nicola church, carved into tuff rock (1459) · small museum
Lunch★ La Grotta da Fiore — cave restaurant at summit. Rabbit Ischian style · bruschetta · local wine. Open daily for lunch. No reservation needed (but call ahead for July).
ReturnSame path back to Fontana (~50min) · CS/CD bus back to Forio · or taxi from Fontana (~€25–28)
Option B — Walk from hotel (more challenging)
RouteFrom hotel, walk uphill through Forio to Santa Maria al Monte (~45min) · then summit trail (~50min)
AllTrailsSanta Maria al Monte → Epomeo · 3.1mi out-and-back · Hard · 1,374ft gain · 2.5–3hr
NoteThe AllTrails page notes this as the highest elevation gain trail on the island — stunning but tough
Logistics & tips
Start earlyLeave hotel by 08:00–08:30 · cooler, less crowded, haze clears for views
KitHiking shoes (mandatory) · 2L water each · sunscreen · light jacket · snacks
ViewsOn a clear day: Capri · Vesuvius · Procida · Ponza · Sorrento peninsula · Naples · Forio below
Summit foodLa Grotta da Fiore: also serves freshly squeezed lemonade and espresso — famous for the rabbit in tuff cave setting
Back byAim to be back in Forio by 15:30–16:00 · recover · sunset aperitivo · dinner 19:45

Scooter / e-bike rental · Forio
Get around independently
The most popular and flexible way to explore Ischia — especially for reaching beaches and viewpoints on your own schedule.
CostScooter rental ~€25–40/day · e-bike ~€20–30/day
LicenceStandard driving licence usually sufficient for 50cc scooters. Ask at rental.
ParkingEasy to find · pay lots in town centres ~€2/hr · free in quieter areas
NoteNo driving licence needed for e-bikes. Heavy summer traffic — allow extra time. Roads can be steep and narrow.
In ForioSeveral rental shops near the port — no need to reserve in advance.
